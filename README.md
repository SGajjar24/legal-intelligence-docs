The **Legal Intelligence Platform** is an enterprise-grade engine designed to solve the "Paperwork Asymmetry" in litigation.

It transforms messy folders of PDFs (Notices, Orders, Appeals) into a **Structured Knowledge Graph**, allowing lawyers to visualize the entire history of a case in seconds.

| **Audit Trail** | every AI claim is linked to the original PDF source. |
